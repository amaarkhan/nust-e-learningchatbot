FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)
Q: Where is NUST ConnectEd?
A: It is the online learning platform of NUST (National University of Sciences and Technology), Pakistan. Website: https://connected.nust.edu.pk
Q: How do I enroll?
A: Click "Enroll Now" on the course page. Pay via debit/credit card (instant enrollment) or HBL bank transfer (up to 48 hours for manual enrollment).
Q: What is the standard course price?
A: Most courses are PKR 7,000. Exceptions: Medical Emergencies (PKR 3,500), Programming Fundamentals for AI & DS (PKR 24,000), HSSC Math Condensed Course (PKR 40,000), Fulbright Program (FREE).
Q: Do I get a certificate?
A: Most courses include a custom certificate upon completion. The HSSC Math certificate is lifetime valid and usable for NUST admissions in future years.
Q: Is the HSSC Math course mandatory for Computing programs?
A: No. It is only mandatory for Pre-Medical students applying to NUST UG Engineering programs. Students applying to BS CS, AI, DS, and BI do NOT need it.
Q: What is the HSSC Math final exam deadline for 2026 admissions?
A: NET-2026 applicants must complete the course and final exam by 25 July 2026.
Q: What happens if I fail the HSSC Math final exam?
A: You can retake it for a fee of PKR 3,000.
Q: Does clearing the HSSC Math course exempt me from NET?
A: No. NET (NUST Entry Test) is still required.
Q: How do I contact NUST ConnectEd?
A: Email: info@connected.nust.edu.pk
